Historical Timeline

1446 AD

Founding of Diriyah by Manaa' Al-Muraydi, establishing the ancestral home of the Saudi royal family

1744 AD

Historic meeting between Imam Mohammed bin Saud and Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulwahhab, establishing the First Saudi State

1818 AD

Ottoman campaign leads to the fall of Diriyah after a six-month siege, ending the First Saudi State

2010 AD

At-Turaif District designated as UNESCO World Heritage Site, recognizing its outstanding universal value
